Q：What is AvgFor?

A：AvgFor is an app developed by USCD students to serve UCSD students.

User do not have to log into mytritonlink every time to check their classes.

By clicking the app on your phone, the information about available seat of your class shows up clearly.

The goal of our team is to create a user friendly interface application in your hand and making the campus life more convenient.

Q：How to add a class?

A：Click add button at top right--> select the class you want to add-->click the lecture session you want to see.

Then you can check the available seats in the seat section.

NOTE: some classes seat information may splited to sections, thus you can not add the lecture to your classes list.

However, if you add one section of a class, all the sections will be shows at seats tab..

Q：How to delete a class?

A：Go to My Class, slide left the class you want to delete, then click delete.

Q：Can I enroll a class through this app?

A：No. This app isn't an official UCSD app, so it doesn't working through your mytritonlink account and won't be able to enroll the class for you

Q：If this page not helping?

A：Slide right and click Feedback, send your problems and advices to us.

We will solve it for you as soon as possible.

Q：What's more?

A：More features are coming soon, keep up! Thanks for your support!

Q：AvgFor 是什么?

A：AvgFor是一个App(手机应用)

AvgFor 由UCSD CSSA 和 Team AvgFor 共同开发 主要目的是为UCSD的学生在课程信息方面提供服务

我们觉得现有的UCSD课程信息网站(Schedule Of Classes, Course Catalogs)使用起来并不是那么方便

尤其是手机用户在访问的时候 界面非常不友好 使用体验不佳

而在每学期选课的时候 因为学生需要频繁的访问这些网站 使得这个问题尤其的突出

AvgFor 是一个专门为手机用户而设计的App 简化了繁琐的操作流程 专注于最核心的课程信息

我们希望能为UCSD学生带来良好的课程信息解决方案 让大家都能轻松的选进自己心仪的课程

Q：AvgFor 怎么用?

A：首先请到 Google Play 搜索"AvgFor" 下载App

App的界面非常简单 主menu有"Seats" "Help" "Feedback" 等

"Seats" 主要显示用户所关注课程的座位信息 方便用户及时调整选课计划 不错过每一个空位

"Seats" 可以通过右上角的添加按钮进行加课 所有学校目前提供的课一览无余

Q：AvgFor 的课程信息是从哪里来的? 座位信息是否准确?

A：AvgFor所有的课程信息都是从UCSD的官网获取而来

我们会在每学期选课信息Avaliable的时候 从UCSD官网获取本学期所有提供的课程

而"Seats"Tab里面所有的课程座位信息都是在用户刷新的时候即时从UCSD官网获取的

不过因为课程信息在动态变化 一切课程信息 还请以UCSD官网的信息为准

Q：AvgFor 能帮我选课么?

A：很抱歉 AvgFor只能提供UCSD课程信息的查询 而无法帮助您选课

选课还请到UCSD官网进行操作

Q：AvgFor 会抓取我的个人信息 比如GPA么?

A：不会 AvgFor的功能只是提供UCSD课程信息的查询

我们没有权限更不会访问您的任何UCSD个人信息 您的GPA和Enrolled Class只有您知道
